Taverna Maria

Step into a world of Greek tradition and hospitality at Taverna Maria in Melinda. Perched above the sea, our taverna invites you to savor the authentic flavors of Greece, from fresh-caught fish to hearty oven-baked dishes that evoke memories of Grandma's kitchen.As you dine, enjoy the serene backdrop of a monumental rock in the shimmering sea and the spectacle of a perfect sunset.

Don't forget to accompany your meal with a glass of ouzo, Greece's iconic spirit, and let the beautiful melodies of Greek music transport you to a place of tranquility and joy.

We eagerly await the pleasure of your company at Taverna Maria, where every bite tells a story of Greek culinary heritage.
